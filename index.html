Our vision

At Simply Subtle, we envision a future where everyday choices are intentional and nourishing, fostering a holistic sense of well-being. Our passion lies in creating premium, nutritious products that not only enhance daily life but also empower people to make thoughtful decisions about their health. We strive to pioneer solutions that promote sustainability and prioritize the well-being of both individuals and communities. By marrying innovation with environmental respect, our goal is to inspire a harmonious balance between what we consume and the planet we inhabit.
This vision drives everything we do—transforming ordinary routines into opportunities for mindful living and positively impacting both personal wellness and the world around us.

About Us

Simply Subtle began as a dream in 2015, when four friends, still in high school, joked about starting a business together. Life took us in different directions, but in 2023, we reunited with a common purpose: to bring innovative, sustainable food products to the world. Founded in January 2024, Simply Subtle was born out of this collective vision. Our journey is just beginning, and with our first product, Date Seed Coffee, launched in September, we’re ready to make a lasting, positive impact on your well-being and the planet.

How to Use

Enjoy your Simply Subtle Date Seed Coffee in just a few simple steps:

Boil 1 cup of milk.

Mix 1.25 tbsp of the Coffee Powder.

Stir and enjoy your hot coffee!

Storage: Keep in a cool, dry place.
